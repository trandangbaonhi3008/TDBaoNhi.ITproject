Beisy - The friend of you

Let's be friend and share your stories

Overview

This app will be an imaginary friend of teenager with introvert and antisocial characteristics, who are vulnerable to depression and suicide. This app is programmed by Python programming language and communicate to people as an app running by iOS. The program will run code to sort words that belong to specific and categorized topic like "depression" "suicide" "sad" "useless" "mental health" The general purpose of the app is to become a friend with those teenagers who desperate to have someone sympathize and react to what they said, in this way the stress level will be cut down and suicide rate will be minimized.

The other purpose is for psychology research, the app will also use track diagram to have repost about cognitive behavior learning of users and reflect their characters which will be used to learn for therapy solution.

Motivation

The psychiatric disorders among Vietnamese and Asian is getting higher and higher yearly, with 30% of the population suffer depression and other mental disorder diseases. According to many reports and statistics from WHO and other well-being organizations, Asian teenagers often found to suffer mental disorders which is the inevitable of stressful education, family problems, bad relationships and being bullied at school. They do not have many friends and close-knit relationship, they rarely can talk to anyone when they desperate for a sharing conversation. Therefore, the idea of spreading the love and care for teenagers who are also in the same age of mine, give something that will make others life better, and heal their inner pain.

Description

Beisy - Desirable accompanion

The slogan of Beisy is “ Be with you all the time”. The main features of the app are about sharing with you and caring for you, which means that you will never get the feeling of being left out. Whenever you feel like you can’t stand no more and there is no one talking to you at that moment, find Beisy.

This is a designate app to send positive and supportive text messages to a user whose expression is mostly belong to psychiatric mental health issues. This imaginary friend is always stay on your phone, and as long as you bring your phone with you, you will have the companion, even online or offline.

Listening and Understanding

Being a close and reliable friend, Beisy will be sincere listener to all of your story and through a system that will evaluate words and semantic of words in the content of massage. She accompanies with you through hardship and happiness, you can tell her all your confidence, and stories that bothered you in many areas of life like love, study, work. In this way, your stress level will significantly deduce. It is significant that lonely teenagers with depression or stress are desperate to find someone who sympathizes with them and sincere reacts to what they said, in such a case, Beisy is that beloved friend of you.

Cause love is on the air

If you sent Beisy a message, she will reply you with a beginning sentence that is “How are you today?” Or “Are you alright now my dear?” and other words which can comfort to the recipient. After analyzing all the text, she will give the appropriated advice or motivation in order to make you feel better. There is a large database of psychology books which can be a good resource for Beisy to give great consulting conversation. Beisy can only solve simple situation, and when it comes to the more complex one she will sort and give you the information of the most fitted psychologist who has the ability to give you the best consultation with professional skills and in-depth knowledge about the psychology field

Personalized your friend as everyoe you want her to become

You can also decide the name of Beisy in the setting mode, virtual companion, and choose a photo of him or her to go along with a customizable profile. Therefore, Beisy is not a pure programmed app since she can be everyone you wish to talk to, such as your crush, your adoslence friend or any kind of person who you want to be in contact with.

HOW DO BEISY WORK ?

The app will receive and store all the message in the cloud storage and then transfer them to the analysis programmed code to process the next step that is dealing with data. After the classificing process by using Naive Bayes method,it is a “ probabilistic classifier and can learn the pattern of examining a set of documents that has been categorized”. The semantic analysis is derived from database where each associated term connects to each other. Then the program will examine their relationship in the ontology. Finally the most important part is stored documents and examine the similarity of words in the same catergorized class that is related to these topics “ depression” “sorrow” sad” and “suicide”.

Reference:Sentiment analysis of twitter data using machine learning approaches and semantic analysis
